Terra

Terra is a mapping app geared towards hikers, climbers, mountaineers. It is intended to make planning for mountain ascents easier as it gives a detailed, 3D view of terrain rather than a flat topo map. It allows users to drop waypoints and draw routes on a 3D terrain map of the world. Users can share their routes with the community and access routes left by others.
